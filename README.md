## In 30 Seconds

We made an **interactive website** that teaches the **"Handshaking Lemma"** - a math concept where 20 people shake hands and we figure out that **18 people** shook hands exactly twice.

---

## The Problem

**20 people at a party**
- Everyone shakes hands
- Total count: 38
- Question: How many shook hands twice?
- **Answer: 18**

---

## What We Created

### **Interactive Website with 7 Steps:**
1. Meet the 20 people
2. Ask the question
3. Explain the secret (each handshake counted 2x)
4. Do the math
5. Write equations
6. Solve it
7. Show answer
